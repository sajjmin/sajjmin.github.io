Camarines Sur Festivals

Camarines Sur is a province located in the Bicol Region of the Philippines, known for its rich cultural heritage, vibrant festivals, and breathtaking natural beauty. Situated in the southeastern part of Luzon, it is a province that offers a perfect blend of tradition and modernity. Camarines Sur is home to warm, hospitable people who take pride in their Bicolano identity.

The culture of Camarines Sur is deeply rooted in religious and cultural practices, with festivals like the Peñafrancia Festival, Kaogma Festival, and Tinagba Festival celebrating the province’s history, faith, and agricultural traditions. These festivals are colorful, lively events filled with street dancing, parades, and rituals that bring the community together in joyous celebration.

Apart from its festivals, Camarines Sur is known for its stunning landscapes, including pristine beaches, majestic mountains, and scenic lakes. Whether it’s exploring the historical landmarks or engaging in exciting outdoor adventures, Camarines Sur truly offers a unique cultural experience that captures the heart of the Filipino spirit.

Festivals in Camarines Sur

Kaogma Festival

History: The Kaogma Festival is celebrated annually in Naga City to honor the culture and traditions of the Bicolanos, featuring a rich blend of history, faith, and community pride.

Date: May 1-5

Activities: Highlights include vibrant parades, traditional cultural dances, and lively street parties that engage the whole city.

Peñafrancia Festival

History: The Peñafrancia Festival honors Our Lady of Peñafrancia, a revered Catholic icon. It is a grand religious event marked by processions and acts of devotion.

Date: September

Activities: The festival features colorful processions, street dancing, and boat races, bringing people from all over the region to Naga City.

Tinagba Festival

History: The Tinagba Festival celebrates the agricultural heritage of the Bicol region, particularly the planting season and honoring the gods for a bountiful harvest.

Date: February 11

Activities: The festival features agricultural exhibitions, a grand parade with colorful floats, and various cultural shows celebrating the harvest.

Peñafrancia Festival: Traslacion Procession

The Peñafrancia Festival is celebrated annually in Naga City, Camarines Sur, in honor of Our Lady of Peñafrancia. The highlight of this festival is the Traslacion Procession, where the image of the Virgin Mary is carried through the streets in a grand procession, accompanied by thousands of devotees. This vibrant religious and cultural event is one of the most important and widely attended festivals in the Bicol region.
